The Process

01

You Pick the Goal

Tell me what you want to work on — or let me watch you hit a few balls first and I'll make the call.

02

We Design the Drill

No generic drills. Every session is structured around your mechanics and the specific pattern you're trying to build.

03

Reps With Feedback

I feed, shadow, or play the target role. Real-time cues keep you correcting in the moment, not guessing later.

04

End-of-session breakdown: what clicked, what still needs reps, and 2–3 things to practice on your own before next time.

Questions

Stuff People
Usually Ask

Are you a certified pickleball coach?

No — and that's worth being upfront about. I'm a 4.0+ rated drilling partner, not a certified instructor. The ideal setup is that you're already working with a coach (or have in the past) and come to me with specific drills or focus areas you want to put reps on. Coaching is expensive; I'm here to give you the time and repetitions to reinforce what you're already learning. Think of me as the person who shows up with a hopper and a plan and keeps the ball coming.

Where do sessions take place?

I come to you. If you have access to a court through an HOA, park, or club, I'll meet you there — any court booking fees are your responsibility. I'm a Picklr member, so if you're also a Picklr member, we can use any Picklr location in Austin and avoid booking fees entirely. I can also help find available public courts if you don't have a spot in mind.

What skill level do you work with?

Primarily 3.0–4.5 players who have the basics and want to drill specific shots. Beginners are welcome but better served by a certified instructor first.

Do I need to bring anything?

Just your paddle and water. I bring a hopper of balls and a tripod if you want to self-record. If you want to use your own balls, that's fine too.

What's your cancellation policy?

Cancel or reschedule with 24 hours notice and there's no charge. Same-day cancellations forfeit the session for pack holders or require payment for drop-ins.

How do I pay?

Venmo or Zelle, same day as the session. Pack purchases upfront. Simple as that — no subscription platforms, no complicated checkout.
